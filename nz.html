Government Formation

Probabilities of Different Outcomes

We consider seven possible outcomes for government formation, from Labour winning a majority on its own, to National doing the same. Obviously other coalitions are plausible, e.g. National could pick NZF over ACT if it could choose between them, but these seven seem most plausible at this time. Every possible election result, assuming no other parties win seats and that National or Labour will lead the next government, falls into exactly one of these outcomes.
